Clan Lore

History of major clans in Control Europe

TCU (The Conquest Uprising)

Made by Flower & Hoody

The first clan ever made was TCU, short for “The Conquest Uprising.” It was created by Flower and Perc70, with another important figure being phoi3xe. The clan peaked at around 150 players but was not favored by event hosters, as no one at the time could compete with organized clan players.

Despite early popularity, it was short lived and became inactive due to poor leadership. Flower later became a peacekeeper. TCU led to the creation of TEP and TVA.

TVA (The Vanguard Alliance)

Made by Hoody

TVA is the root of modern-day Valhalla. Founded by Domikel, it produced many top players including Elite, Bluevsavitar, Sokk, zk, Hoody, and Flame.

The clan was extremely toxic and rule-breaking, leading to conflicts with Rozetina. This resulted in its deletion, ending its short but impactful existence.

TSO (1)

Made by Hoody

TSO marked the real beginning of modern CE clans. It was the first lasting clan and introduced structured roles like People's General.

Early elections included Hoody, zk, and phoi3xe. Hoody campaigned heavily, using advertising and alt accounts to influence new members.

TSO (2)

Made by Hoody

After internal conflict, Dahmer Squad (TSO2) formed. Leadership included Hoody, Flame, Elite, and Sokk. Peacekeepers intervened and banned many members.

Hoody later infiltrated Dahmer Squad and caused its destruction from within. Around the same time, Pub Clan rose through event host influence.

Vanguard

Made by Hoody

Vanguard became the most dominant clan in CE history. It introduced structured 1v1s, skill tests, and competitive leaderboards.

With top players like Floppa, Mattie, Flame, and others, Vanguard rarely lost. Eventually, it split into Valhalla and Valour before being shut down.

Valour

Made by Ryaan

Originally a joke clan, Valour became competitive after Vanguard’s fall. It hosted early FFA skill tests and grew to over 70 members.

Due to inactivity and leadership limits, Valour eventually merged into Valhalla.

AoS (Alliance of Sovereign)

Made by Aurza

AoS began as a small friend group and evolved into a competitive clan. It gained notable members and won the first cross-clan 1v1 against Valhalla.

The clan ended after Floppa stepped down and later merged into Valhalla.

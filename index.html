Clan Lore

History of major clans in Control Europe

TCU (The Conquest Uprising)

Made by Flower & Hoody

The first clan that was ever made was called TCU, which stood for “The Conquest Uprising.” It was created by two old players, Flower and Perc70, with another important person being phoi3xe, who will come up later. This clan peaked at around 150 players. It wasn't favored by event hosters cause at the time no one was skilled enough in CE to beat people from a clan who actually knew what they were doing, Being the first clan, it was relatively easy to gain popularity, but it was also very short lived

It became a very dead clan with poor leadership since flower had become a peacekeeper. There isn’t much more to say about it, but it did lead to the creation of two other notable clans: TEP and TVA. I will focus on TVA because TEP was discontinued quickly due to lack of popularity and eventually merged back with TCU. Perc70 and Flower never returned to clans, and it was time for something new.

TVA (The Vanguard Alliance)

Made by Hoody

TVA (The Vanguard Alliance) is the complete root of modern-day Valhalla. It was the first in a long chain of clans and was founded by another old player, Domikel. Many notable members came from TVA, including Elite, Bluevsavitar, Sokk, zk, Hoody, and Flame, all of whom stayed in the game for a while and became top players.

TVA was extremely toxic and rule-breaking, which led to Domikel getting into fights with Rozetina. This ultimately resulted in either the deletion of the server or the permanent ban of all TVA members. Domikel decided to delete the clan, ending TVA’s short but impactful existence.

TSO (1)

Made by Hoody

TSO marks the real start of CE lore. It was the first modern clan and the first one to actually last. The clan was formed shortly after TVA’s deletion and Domikel’s separation from CE. Bluesavitar and Elite were co-owners.

This is where peacekeepers began cracking down on promotion and advertisement, leading to a more focused clan centered around prominent members rather than randoms. Sokk, zk, and phoi3xe were all given valuable roles within the clan. phoi3xe was named the first People’s General, a position that had elections from time to time.

The first election for People’s General had three important candidates: Hoody, zk, and phoi3xe. Hoody was the most popular due to his in-game title, but it was still a close race between him and phoi3xe. phoi3xe campaigned by advertising in the main server, making alts, and spreading lies to new members.

The election lasted around three days. On the final day, when Hoody was in the lead and most notable members were offline, phoi3xe used the administrator role he still held to delete every channel, ban his competitors, and DM everyone a server invite before banning them from the clan. Only the owner, Bluesavitar, remained. The server invite was to a clan called The Dahmer Squad, led by Ianzz, with traitors from TSO who had planned with phoi3xe to “nuke” the clan.

TSO (2)

Made by Hoody

The first person reinvited to TSO2 was Hoody, who was given a top role, along with Flame, Elite, and Sokk. It was hard to regain members, and they were very angry with phoi3xe. Around this time, the new peacekeepers were elected in the main server. One of them, Anh, helped by banning all top members of Dahmer Squad and the traitors.

However, Dahmer Squad still had influence and members. Hoody decided to infiltrate it and manipulate members—including Rotgel, zk, and a few others—into nuking their own clan, resulting in its destruction.

While TSO2 was regaining members, a new event host, Coby, created a clan called Pub Clan. Using his influence, he advertised in the main server and at events without punishment, making Pub Clan bigger and more dominant than TSO2. Rotgel even joined them.

Hoody and Sokk were the primary leaders of TSO2, with Bluesavitar inactive. Hoody decided to create a new clan to defeat Pub and recruit every competitive CE player. His right-hand man was Floppa, who was rising to become one of the best event players at the time.

Vanguard

Made by Hoody

With Hoody creating Vanguard, he ended TSO2, destroying his second clan, and began mass advertisement. The peacekeepers favored him more now.

Hoody also created the first official 1v1s and skill tests, including Ukraine vs France, and the competitive scene quickly developed, along with a leaderboard.

Vanguard became the biggest clan, with Hoody recruiting all previous members from Pub and TSO2. Floppa dominated almost every event alongside Mattie, Flame, Georgy, Zhukov, and Comrade Doggie. Vanguard rarely lost, with only Rotgel and Boris as consistent competition.

Event hosts tried to curb Vanguard’s dominance by creating rules against it, but it kept growing, especially through training all its players. The global leaderboard resembled a Vanguard leaderboard more than anything. Notable members included MVV, S29, Bonekoko, Comrade Doggie, DZK, Georgy, Nexus, Bondt, Babatunde, and Richard.

Vanguard had no real rivals. Competitive play mostly occurred within the clan, with players competing against each other. Eventually, Hoody stepped down. Before closing Vanguard, two new clans were created by top members: S29 formed Valhalla, and Bondt formed Valour, which became rival successor clans. Hoody closed Vanguard for good and kept its server hidden, promoting both new clans before officially resigning from CE.

Valour

Made by Ryaan

After meeting people in the server Duck Empire, Bondt created a joke clan/friend group called Lalalala Clan, including Ryaan, Axel, and Aizen as notable members.

After Vanguard fell, Bondt saw an opportunity to make the joke clan serious and renamed it Valour. During its first week, he hosted FFA battle royals as skill tests. The leaderboard consisted of eight players: Bond, Ryaan, Axel, Aizen, Soviet Cat, NSA, and Ducky.

The clan eventually had 70+ members, but only 6–15 were active. Bondt did not have enough time to manage the clan. After discussions with Ryaan and other members, he decided to close Valour and merge with Valhalla, Vanguard’s successor.

Some remaining members attempted to create a clan called Zenith, which quickly failed, and they eventually joined Valhalla as well.

AoS (Alliance of Sovereign)

Made by Aurza

AoS began as a small friend group with the founding members being Aurza, Floppa, Nava, Nebula, Pedru It gained notable members and won the first cross-clan 1v1 against Valhalla.

The group was originally named the "Silly Fish Alliance" All members wore the Roblox marketplace bundle "The Fish" Later the group changed the name to Alliance of Sovereign (AoS). New members did not own the bundle and disliked the original name. After that, AoS gained several trusted and influential members including, Ananas, Mattie, BondT, Enel, Mirke, Sai, Ducks, Raze, These are trusted members only.

AoS ended after leader Floppa stepped down. The clan later merged with Valhalla, which led to the alliance being abandoned.
